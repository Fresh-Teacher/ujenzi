WHO WE ARE

Ujenzi is a Group of Companies with partnership of JEI Holdings, JEI Petroleum, JEI investments, JEI Agro Processing and Athera Naturals.

We are dedicated to transforming your vision into reality. With our expertise in construction, real estate, agriculture and value addition, finance, and business, we offer a comprehensive range of services to cater to your diverse needs.

Whether you're an individual looking for a new home or a company seeking reliable construction solutions, we have the knowledge and experience to deliver exceptional results.
