WHO WE ARE

Ujenzi is not just a construction company; it is a dynamic Group of Companies driven by the spirit of innovation and collaboration. In partnership with JEI Holdings, JEI Petroleum, JEI Investments, JEI Agro Processing, and Athera Naturals, we have woven a tapestry of excellence across diverse industries.

We are more than builders; we are visionaries dedicated to transforming dreams into reality. Our expertise spans construction, real estate, agriculture and value addition, finance, and business. Ujenzi offers a comprehensive range of services, tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Whether you're an individual seeking a new home or a company in search of reliable construction solutions, Ujenzi stands as your trusted partner. Our unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional results, combined with a wealth of experience, sets us apart in the industry.

Mission

Our mission is to help humanity thrive by enabling all teams to work together effortlessly. We believe in creating spaces and opportunities that contribute to the well-being and progress of individuals and communities.

Vision

Our vision is to serve our customers better, to always be relevant in their lives, and to form lifelong relationships. We aspire to be the go-to solution provider in construction, real estate, and allied industries, consistently exceeding expectations.

Values

At Ujenzi, our values guide every action and decision:

  • Commitment: We are committed to delivering excellence, meeting deadlines, and exceeding expectations.

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every project, fostering innovation and continuous improvement.

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with integrity, honesty, and transparency, building trust with our clients and partners.
